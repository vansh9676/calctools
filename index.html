Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to use online financial calculators?

Absolutely. CalcTools operates entirely on the client-side (in your browser). We do not store, record, or transmit your financial data to any server. Your privacy is our priority.

How accurate are the SIP returns?

SIP calculators assume a constant rate of return (e.g., 12%) to project future value. Real market returns fluctuate. However, over long periods (10+ years), equity markets in India have historically delivered 12-14% CAGR.
