Understanding Percentages

A percentage is a number or ratio expressed as a fraction of 100. It is often denoted using the percent sign "%". Percentages are used to express proportions, rates, and comparisons.

Basic Percentage

X% of Y = (X/100) × Y

To find X percent of Y, multiply Y by X/100.

Percentage Increase

Increase = ((New - Original)/Original) × 100%

Calculate how much a value has increased.

Percentage Decrease

Decrease = ((Original - New)/Original) × 100%

Calculate how much a value has decreased.

Percentage Difference

Difference = |(V1 - V2)|/((V1 + V2)/2) × 100%

Compare two different values.
