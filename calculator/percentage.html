What Is a Percentage?

A percentage represents a number expressed as a fraction of 100. It is widely used to compare values, show growth or decline, and simplify ratios in everyday calculations.

How Percentage Is Calculated

The basic percentage formula is: (Part ÷ Whole) × 100. This calculator applies the formula instantly to avoid manual errors.

Real-Life Uses of Percentages

  • Exam scores and academic results
  • Discounts during shopping and sales
  • GST, tax, and financial growth calculations
  • Profit and loss analysis
  • Statistical and survey results

Example Percentage Calculation

If you score 420 marks out of 500, your percentage is:

(420 ÷ 500) × 100 = 84%

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Confusing percentage increase with percentage points
  • Using the wrong base value
  • Rounding too early in calculations
