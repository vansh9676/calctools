About Age Calculation

This calculator determines your exact age by calculating the difference between two dates. It accounts for varying month lengths and leap years to provide accurate results.

The calculation considers the exact number of days in each month and accounts for leap years (years divisible by 4, except for years divisible by 100 unless also divisible by 400).
