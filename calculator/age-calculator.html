Age Calculator

This age calculator helps you calculate your age based on your date of birth. It is useful for general planning and educational purposes.

Your Age:

How Age Is Calculated

Age is calculated by comparing your date of birth with the current date. The calculation considers completed years, remaining months, and days based on the calendar system.

This calculator automatically adjusts for different month lengths and leap years, providing a more accurate age breakdown than simple year-based calculations.

Where Age Calculation Is Commonly Used

  • Government exams and eligibility checks
  • School and college admissions
  • Job applications with age limits
  • Official documents such as passports and IDs
  • Personal planning and milestone tracking

Example of Age Calculation

If a person is born on 1 January 2000 and today’s date is 1 March 2026, their age would be:

  • 26 years
  • 2 months
  • 0 days

Important Notes About Age Results

Age calculations may vary slightly depending on how institutions define age cut-off dates. Some organizations consider only completed years, while others include exact months and days.

Always verify official age requirements using the relevant authority’s guidelines.
