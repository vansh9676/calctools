Understanding BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a person's weight in kilograms divided by the square of height in meters. BMI is an inexpensive and easy screening method for weight category: underweight, healthy weight, overweight, and obesity.

BMI Categories

Category BMI Range Risk Level
Underweight < 18.5 Increased risk
Normal weight 18.5 - 24.9 Lowest risk
Overweight 25 - 29.9 Increased risk
Obesity Class I 30 - 34.9 High risk
Obesity Class II 35 - 39.9 Very high risk

Note: BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnostic tool. It does not account for muscle mass, bone density, overall body composition, and racial and sex differences. Please consult a healthcare professional for a proper health assessment.
