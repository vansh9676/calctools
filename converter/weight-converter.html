Weight Conversion Reference

Metric System

  • Milligram (mg): 1/1000 of a gram
  • Gram (g): Base unit of weight in metric system
  • Kilogram (kg): 1000 grams
  • Metric Ton (t): 1000 kilograms

Imperial System

  • Ounce (oz): 1/16 of a pound
  • Pound (lb): 16 ounces
  • Stone (st): 14 pounds
