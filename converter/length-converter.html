Length Conversion Reference

Metric System

  • Millimeter (mm): 1/1000 of a meter
  • Centimeter (cm): 1/100 of a meter
  • Meter (m): Base unit of length in SI
  • Kilometer (km): 1000 meters

Imperial System

  • Inch (in): 1/12 of a foot
  • Foot (ft): 12 inches
  • Yard (yd): 3 feet
  • Mile (mi): 1760 yards

Conversion Formulas

1 cm = 10 mm Centimeters to millimeters
1 m = 100 cm Meters to centimeters
1 km = 1000 m Kilometers to meters
1 in = 2.54 cm Inches to centimeters
1 ft = 30.48 cm Feet to centimeters
1 mi = 1.609 km Miles to kilometers
