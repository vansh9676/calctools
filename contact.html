Frequently Asked Questions

Are your calculators really free?

Yes! All calculators and converters on CalcTools are completely free to use. No registration, no subscriptions, no hidden fees.

Do you store my calculation data?

No. All calculations are performed locally in your browser. We don't store or transmit your calculation inputs or results to our servers.

Can I use your calculators for commercial purposes?

Our tools are designed for personal and educational use. For commercial use, please contact us for licensing information.

How accurate are your converters?

We strive for maximum accuracy. Currency converters use reliable exchange rate data, and unit converters use standard conversion factors. However, always verify critical conversions.

Can I suggest a new calculator?

Absolutely! We're always looking to add useful tools. Please use the contact form or email us with your suggestion.

Do you have mobile apps?

Our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile devices. No app installation needed - just visit our site in your mobile browser.

Need Immediate Help?

For common issues, check our FAQ section above. Most questions can be answered there.

If you're experiencing technical issues with a specific calculator, please include the following in your message:

  • Which calculator you're using
  • What inputs you entered
  • What error or issue you're experiencing
  • Your browser and device information
