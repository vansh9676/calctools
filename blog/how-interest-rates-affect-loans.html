How Interest Rates Affect Loans and EMIs

Educational guide · Finance basics

Interest rate is one of the most important factors when taking a loan. Even a small change in interest rate can significantly impact your monthly EMI and the total amount you repay over time.

What Is an Interest Rate?

An interest rate is the cost charged by lenders for borrowing money. It is usually expressed as an annual percentage of the loan amount.

How Interest Rate Affects EMI

Higher interest rates increase your EMI or extend your loan tenure. Lower interest rates reduce the overall cost of borrowing.

Example: Small Rate Change, Big Impact

A loan of ₹10 lakh at 8% interest for 20 years costs much less than the same loan at 9%. Over long tenures, the difference can run into lakhs.

Fixed vs Floating Interest Rates

Fixed rates remain constant, while floating rates change with market conditions. Each has advantages depending on economic trends.

Why Understanding Interest Rates Matters

Borrowers who understand interest rates can choose better loan offers, reduce total interest paid, and plan finances more responsibly.
