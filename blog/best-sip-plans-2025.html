Best Tip for Beginners

Start early and stay invested longer. SIP benefits grow significantly after 5+ years. Also do not stop SIPs during market correction – that’s when units are cheaper!

FAQs on SIP Plans

1️⃣ Which SIP is best for beginners?
Large-cap or balanced advantage funds offer stable growth with lower volatility.

2️⃣ What is the minimum SIP amount?
You can start with just ₹100 per month.

3️⃣ How long should I invest?
Minimum 5–10 years for best compounding effect.

Final Verdict: Start with low-risk bluechip/balanced SIPs → increase amount slowly every year!
